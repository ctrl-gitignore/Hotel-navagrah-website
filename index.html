Our Story

Nine Planets,
One Sanctuary

Hotel Navagrah draws its name from the nine celestial bodies — Nava meaning nine, Grah meaning planet. Each corner of our hotel carries the spirit of a different planet: the warmth of the Sun, the calm of the Moon, the strength of Mars. We don't just offer rooms. We offer an experience that feels written in the stars.


Nestled in the heart of the city, Navagrah has been a landmark of gracious hospitality for over two decades — serving guests who value quiet elegance over loud luxury.
